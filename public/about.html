About Jill Diaz

Welcome to my corner of the internet! I'm Jill Diaz, a passionate individual embarking on an exciting journey into the world of web development. At 53 years young, I'm proof that it's never too late to pursue your dreams and dive into new adventures.

For the past two decades, my focus has been on raising a family and cherishing the joys of being a stay-at-home mom and grandma. While those years were incredibly rewarding, I've always had a lingering passion for technology and design, particularly in the realm of web development.

Recently, I made the decision to enroll in a web development boot camp, eager to update my skills and carve out a new path for myself in the ever-evolving landscape of tech. With a curious mind and a determination to learn, I've been immersing myself in the world of HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and all things frontend.

Returning to the job force after such a significant hiatus may seem daunting to some, but for me, it's an exhilarating opportunity to reignite my career and pursue a newfound passion. I'm excited to apply my creativity, problem-solving skills, and attention to detail to create beautiful and user-friendly web experiences.

As I continue on this journey, I'm not just building websites; I'm building a future filled with endless possibilities. Join me as I embrace the challenges, celebrate the victories, and showcase the fruits of my labor in the world of frontend web development.

Thank you for stopping by, and I invite you to follow along as I write the next chapter of my story in code.
